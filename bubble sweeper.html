ℹ️How to Play

  • Left-click to reveal a cell.
  • Right-click to flag a potential bubble.
  • Reveal every safe cell to win the round.
  • Numbers show how many bubbles touch the cell.
  • Avoid popping a bubble or the board will flood!
  • Need help? Use the hint button to highlight a safe move.
